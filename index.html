GCSI @ MXD 2023

About the Event

The Global Cyber Security Initiative (GCSI), annual cyber security summit, is the go-to event to help you gain more knowledge on the world of cybersecurity. This event is co-hosted with MxD (Manufacturing x Digital), Illinois Institute of Technology (IIT) and Pan Asian American Business Council (PAABC). This event will bring together more than 200 high-level cybersecurity leaders, senior executives, C-level executives, CISOs, compliance specialists, and security officers for sessions focused on Cyber Security, Strategy, and Risk & Budgeting. If you are interested in gaining more insights on cybersecruty and latest technology we invite you to come in and enjoy.
